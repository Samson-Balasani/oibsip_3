Sundar Pichai

Sundar is the CEO of Google and Alphabet and serves on Alphabet's Board of Directors. He joined Google in 2004 and helped lead the development of Google Toolbar and then Google Chrome, which grew to become the world's most popular internet browser.

Interesting facts about Sundar Pichai

  • Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972.
  • In 2004, Pichai joined Google in the product management and innovation field
  • Pichai studied his undergraduate degree in Metallurgical Engineering back in India.
  • The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania awarded him an MBA
  • Sundar Pichai became Google’s CEO in 2015.
  • After his appointment as Alphabet’s headman in 2019, Pichai’s net worth soared, currently reaching $1.44 billion.
  • Sundar Pichai and his wife, Anjali Pichai, have two children.
